# FAQ: Account & Trading

## Account Opening
**Q: What is the minimum deposit?**
A: The minimum initial deposit for a standard brokerage account is $1,000 USD.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Identity verification (KYC) typically takes 1-2 business days.

## Trading Fees
- **Stocks & ETFs**: $0 commission.
- **Options**: $0.65 per contract.
- **Margin Rate**: 8.5% APR for balances under $25,000.

## Withdrawals
**Q: Are there withdrawal fees?**
A: ACH transfers are free. Wire transfers incur a $25 fee.
**Q: What is the settlement period?**
A: Stock trades settle in T+1 business days. You can withdraw cash after it settles.
